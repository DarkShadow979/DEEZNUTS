Coming Soon, Soundbored!




Soundbored is an upcoming soundboard app where you'll be able to mix your own sounds!

Most soundboards available for purchase or download are usually restricted to a given set of sounds and nothing more, while other customizable soundboards have certain limitations or are poorly designed. With Soundbored, you'll be able to mix and match your favorite sounds (.ogg, .mp3. or .wav) while having a easy to navigate and pleasant looking UI. Soundbored also allows you to customize how your soundboard looks. You can change the size of the buttons, change the backgrounds, and even use images as icons(.jpg, .png, .bmp)! With SoundBored, you'll be able to tailor your soundboards to your liking, and you'll never be bored!
